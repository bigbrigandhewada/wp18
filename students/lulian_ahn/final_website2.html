1st Week

As an introductory class, we were just to understand the basics of HTML. As a take home assigment, we were assigned to design 5 different shapes applying the basic knowledge learned from our first class.

2nd Week

After covering the foundation of HTML, we learned CSS and using grids. As a take home assigment, we were assigned to create collage designs using the grid system we have learnt.

3rd Week

We started to explore the usage of flexbox and interacting with the page using jquery and such. As a take home assignment, we were assigned to make a interactive page.

4th Week

Incorporating web fonts and editing svg files through coding was introduced. As a take home assigment, we were assigned to make a website.
